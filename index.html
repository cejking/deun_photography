About Us

Hello, we are Fleur and Wendy, the owners of Deun Photography, a professional photography company based in Dubai. We are both professionally trained and highly experienced photographers, that have covered a variety of shots throughout our professional careers.

At Deun we specialize in a wide array of photography needs, from interiors to family photography, you can learn more about the types of photography.

We aim to focus on the needs of our clients and pride ourselves on our personal, flexible, and professional approach when working with you. We aspire to make our shoots creative and fun, as we believe the enjoyment of the shoot is as much an integral part of the image as the final print itself.
